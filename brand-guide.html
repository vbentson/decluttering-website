Color Palette

The palette is built around a warm, peachy ivory base with soft coral as the lead action color. Golden amber and dusty sage support the warmth. Deep grove anchors the brand in nature. Teal is used sparingly as an energizing pop — pulled directly from the logo.

04

Brand Mood & Feel

Grounded Space Co. sits at the intersection of high-end interior aesthetics and genuine human warmth. The brand should feel like a trusted, beautiful friend who happens to be very good at what they do.

Unhurried Californian Warm Grounded Tactile 5 PM light Intentional Elevated but approachable October afternoon Clean windows Soft glow Not a sunset — the hour before

The brand IS

  • ✦   Warm without being rustic
  • ✦   Premium without being cold
  • ✦   Earthy without being muddy
  • ✦   Editorial without being unapproachable
  • ✦   Colorful without being loud

The brand is NOT

  • —   Stark minimalist / Scandinavian cold
  • —   Boho or overly eclectic
  • —   Corporate or institutional
  • —   Hot pink / neon energy
  • —   Generic "cleaning service" blue

Logo Reference

GROUNDED SPACE CO.

The logo's four dots — orange, gold, teal, green — are the anchor for all color decisions. This style guide translates those into a fully applied design system while preserving the logo's warm, playful grounding energy.
